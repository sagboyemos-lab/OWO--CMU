History Of Owo CMU

History of the formation of Co-operative thrift and credit society in Owo was dated back to 11/9/60 when the first society Owo Ilosiwaju C.T.C.S.Ltd. was Established by Mr S.A Falohun and Mr M.L Amaka. Thier lofty idea did not end there for with the full backing of the erst co-operative legend and the father of co-operation in Owo Chief S.A Adeloye they went further in 1961. What is today known as Owo C.M.U.Ltd. was inagurated inside the hall of Owo C.P.M.S.Ltd. on friday 14th July 1961 at a meeting Presided over by Chief S.A Adeloye of blessed memory and and it was then christianed Owo C.T.C.U.Ltd.

The Historical meeting was attended by 4 delegates from each of the 5 registered and 5 unregistered societies in the area. The achitects of the Union Chief S.A. Adeloye, Mr S.A. Falodun who was Co-operative inspector and Mr. M.L. Amaka who was then the F/Secretary for owo was also present.
